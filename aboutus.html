About

At Neelkanth, we do more than just publish B.Tech solved papers and ITI textbooks or polytechnic textbooks or engineering textbooks. We call ourselves knowledge and skill providers who light the path for millions of students and help them to create a future of their dreams. We pride ourselves in providing affordable products whether they are engineering solved papers or ITI handbooks or polytechnic solved papers or B.Sc solved papers; which is quite a feat in today’s burgeoning costs! Our race to the top is not just about numbers but also about satisfied and smiling educators, parents, students and children. Neelkanth believes that its most valuable assets are its people. That is why we believe in enhancing individual achievement, promoting professional growth in a diverse and inclusive environment where innovation is a habit.

Corporate Profile

Neelkanth Publishers (P) Ltd. is one of the foremost and fastest growing publishers and distributors in India. Ever since its inception in 2005, in Jaipur, Rajasthan, it has been striving to enhance the quality of education in various technical (Engineering, ITI, BCA, and MCA) and non-technical (BBA and MBA) fields. In the last twelve years, the company has not only grown from strength to strength but also earned the respect of educators and students alike. The B.Tech solved papers, ITI handbooks, Polytechnic solved papers and ITI textbooks published by the company are recommended in well-known universities and ITIs all over the country. We also have an expansive distribution network which covers pan India. Buoyed by its phenomenal success and ever-increasing popularity, the company plans to now expand its horizons and foray into other domains of education. Whether they are B.Tech solved papers, ITI handbooks or ITI textbooks, the books published by Neelkanth are not only about quality content but also have a simplistic approach which allows students to easily understand the most complex concepts. Affordable costs, superior illustrations and diagrams, plenty of solved and unsolved examples and eloquent explanations further enhance the teaching-learning experience.

Our Achievements

  • We have published more than 10,000 copies of various skill development books for MG Career Park and its branches pan India, under the PMKVY scheme for about 8 sector skill councils for courses such as Plumbing, Hand-Embroiderer, Cutting and Sewing, Loader Unloader, Warehouse Picker, Warehouse Packer, Goods Packaging Machine Operator, Inventory Clerk, Field Technician (Computing and Peripherals).

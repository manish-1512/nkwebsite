Q1.  How can I become a NK Guru? What does it involve?

NK Gurus are basically subject matter experts (SME) who are great at solving questions related to their field of expertise. So, in case you can solve questions from B.Tech branches such as Civil, Electrical, Electronics, Mechanical, Computers and so on, you can register as a NK Guru. Once you register, you will be given a sample question from your subject (which will be specified while registering). The sample question shall be an unpaid question (unless otherwise specified). You need to solve the sample question and submit the solution (you can either write it and scan it or type it and send the word document). Once the sample question is approved, you will be eligible to bid on the questions posted on the website. Every question has a certain deadline and certain price. Deadline adherence is mandatory, failing which there will be a deduction in payment.
